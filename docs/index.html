Research statement

My research focuses on Computer Vision and Machine Learning. I am particularly interested in how CV and ML can be used to improve our lives in areas such as productivity and accessibility. I want to investigate potential solutions to some of the ethical problems in AI and how to make AI better at problem solving without sacrificing morality.
